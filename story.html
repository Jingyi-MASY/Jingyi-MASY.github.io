First Date


At 11: 40 AM June 30th 2019

Jingyi was in a sleeveless cyan dress at the southeast gate of campus. This is the first time Mingxuan asked Jingyi out. She arrived slightly earlier than the appointed time. The day before, Mingxuan took a new haircut. He thought of a few topics to arouse her interest. He checked activities and restaurants she may enjoy. But the moment he saw her face, he looked pretty clumsy. 4 hours later, she became his girlfriend. All the anxiety proved to be unnecessary. They are made for each other.
